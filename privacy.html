Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025

At GirleeBag.com, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of your personal information. We believe in transparency, especially as our products are enjoyed by growing young ladies and their families.

Safety Note for Parents: While GirleeBag products are designed for girls and ladies aged 8 to 28, this website is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect following types of information when you interact with us:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, shipping/billing address, phone number, and payment details (processed securely via our trusted third-party partners).
  • Automatic Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and site usage patterns via cookies.
  • Order History: Record of your purchases, preferences, and shopping cart contents.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data allows us to provide a premium shopping experience. We use it to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders with speed and accuracy.
  • Provide helpful customer support.
  • Send you updates on your orders or opted-in marketing promotions.
  • Personalize your experience on our site.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

3. Data Sharing

We value your trust. We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share data with essential service providers (shipping carriers, payment processors, and analytics partners) who are strictly required to protect your information.

4. Cookies & Personalization

We use cookies to remember your preferences and analyze how our site is used. You can manage these settings through your browser, though some site features may require cookies to function correctly.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights or opt out of marketing, please reach out to us via our contact page.

6. Security Measures

We implement robust technical and administrative safeguards, including SSL encryption for all transactions. While we strive for 100% security, please remember that no digital transmission is entirely risk-free.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

GirleeBag.com does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not attempt to register for our website or send any personal information to us. In the event that we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately. We encourage parents to be present during their child's browsing experience.

8. Contact Us

For any questions regarding your privacy, please visit our contact page.
